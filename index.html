About Beeing About

Beeing About was established in 2015.
Since then we have travelled to the West, South, North and East of Ireland, discovering the best off the beaten track routes. We have also recorded those travels, and made them into bite-size souvenirs, so we can re-live them once again. Often we like to return to those places, and the travelogs we have created help us decide where to go next.

We are now bringing this experience to you. If you would like us to design Iterinary, record your own holiday documentary or maybe both, get in touch, let's discuss our offer. Maybe you have bundles of holiday video recorded and now is the time to put them on display, let us edit those onto your own travelogs. We look forward to hearing from you.
